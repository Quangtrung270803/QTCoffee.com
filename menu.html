Strong Coffee, Strong Roots About Our Cafe

The coffee menu at QT COFFEE is a perfect combination of our signature coffees and our unique recipes. All products are made from selected coffee beans and served by dedicated, experienced staff.
If you are a fan of traditional coffee, try typical black coffee dishes like Espresso, Americano or Cappuccino. If you want to try something more exciting, we also offer cold brew coffees like Cold Brew, Nitro Cold Brew and creamy latte.

In addition the coffee menu of QT COFFEE , also includes many other drinks such as tea, juice, and cake. All are made with fresh ingredients and served with the finest quality..

Strong Cake, Strong Roots About Our Cake

QT COFFEE's cake menu is a perfect combination of sophistication and delicious taste. We offer a wide range of handmade cakes, with fresh ingredients and without the use of any preservatives or artificial flavors. You will be captivated by delicious cakes such as fruit cake, tiramisu cake, matcha cake, chocolate cake and many more cakes. All beautifully prepared and decorated by our talented team of chefs. .

In addition , we also offer gluten-free cakes , to meet the needs of those on a diet or those with gluten allergies.
